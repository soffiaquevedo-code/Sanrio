The Creation of the Sanrio Company

1960 - 1970

Yamanashi Silk Center Co. was established in Tokyo with the aim of establishing a social communication buisness. Launched their first gift product "Strawberry". then published the poetry collection "Loving Songs". Afterwards they established the Sanrio Greeting Co.

1970 - 1980

First store was opened in Shinjuku, Tokyo. Then 2 years later the name changed to Sanrio Company. The first characters Hello Kity, Patty and Jimmy were created. Throught the years they created more characters, and opened more stores around Asia.

1980 - 2010

They were listed top 3 in the Tokyo Stock Exchange, they then relocated their head office in Osaki, Shinagawa, Tokyo, and had their stores located worldwide. Then later they opened their theme park "Harmonyland". Afterwards more characters were created like Cinnamoroll and Sugarbunnies

2010 - 2022

More stores and theme parks were opened worldwide, having a big impact in Asia. Created shows and movies which was a big hit, and created around 15 new characters. Around the 2000's Tomokuni Tsuji was appointed as the President and CEO of Sanrio. Then Sintaro Tsuji was appointed as the Founder and Chairman of Sanrio. They then formed a capital alliance with YARUKI Switch Group Holdings Inc.
