1960-1970 - Yamanashi Silk Center Co. was established in Tokyo with the aim of establishing a social communication buisness. Launched their first gift product "Strawberry". then published the poetry collection "Loving Songs". Afterwards they established the Sanrio Greeting Co.

1970-1980 - First store was opened in Shinjuku, Tokyo. Then 2 years later the name changed to Sanrio Company. The first characters Hello Kity, Patty and Jimmy were created. Throught the years they created more characters, and oepned more stores around Asia.

1980-2000 They were listed top 3 in the Tokyo Stock Exchange, they then relocated their head office in Osaki, Shinagawa, Tokyo, and had their stores located worldwide. Then laster they opened their theme park "Harmonyland". Afterwards more characters were created like Cinnamoroll and Sugarbunnies
