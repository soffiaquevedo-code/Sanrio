POCHACCO

Pochacco, is a chubby white dog character, he is known for his black floppy ears. He was initially called "The Yorimichi Dog", Pochacco's name comming from the Japanese word "pocha pocha," meaning "chubby". From the start, Pochacco was positioned as an active and friendly character, often participating in various sports and activities, setting him apart from other characters cuteness alone.

Minoru Onoue created Pochacco, his fisrt appereance being in February 29, 1989.
