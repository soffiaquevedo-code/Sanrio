Throught my years in Holycross I was also able to discover my passion for design and programming, leading me to join the Computer Programming class, with Mr Francia as my teacher. For my website I chose to do it about Sanrio, because I am a big fan of many of their characters. I often find myself wanting to learn more about them, but it's hard to find a single website with all the info I'm loooking for. This inspired me to make my own website on Sanrio characters and putting all the info about them in one place. While keeping in mind on making it easy to navigate for anyone.
